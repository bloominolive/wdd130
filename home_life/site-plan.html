Overview

Purpose

When was the last time you changed your air filter in your home? When was the last time you cleaned out your gutters? Have you checked your dryer vents recently? Trying to keep up with home maintenance or even daily to-do's can be overwhelming and time can easily slip away from us with our everyday routines. My website is a solution to keep all your home and life maintenance tasks organized so you never have to wake up in the middle of the night and realize you haven't resealed your grout for the past 5 years.

Audience

My audience will likely consist of mothers between the ages of 25-50 who own a home. It is also likely they would be married and have children. I want this site to be usable by the family, though, not just a mom, so it will cater to men and women both.

Content

Home page

The Home Hub
The Home Hub will be on the home page of our site. This will be a spot where users can view seasonal featured topics, ideas for general life motivation or improvement, and recommended tasks for that day. The Home Hub will also provide top weekly cleaning tips. From here, users can navigate to the other pages including a blog and calendar page. This page will have the top things users will want to know about keeping their life organized and home maintained. It should not be too busy so as to deter from the main focuses and links to other pages for more details.

Images for the Home page

Blog

The Blog
The Blog will be a source of inspiration and information for anyone who needs to tackle the challenges that come with being a homeowner. This site will be updated at least weekly with articles on how to successfully accomplish to-dos for the home, keeping organized in life, having a maintenance plan, having an emergency plan, and more. This page will be the life of our site.

Images for the Blog

Calendar

Calendar
The calendar page will give an idea of what days should have what tasks for their home or life. It will show them how often to perform suggested tasks and be easy to print for the month with extra space on the days to write in their own custom to-do lists.
