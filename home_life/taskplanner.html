Task Planner

Get Started

Daily

  • Sweep floors
  • Wipe bathroom and kitchen countertops
  • One load of laundry (and get it put away!)
  • No dirty dishes at the end of the day
  • 10 minute declutter
  • Prayer/Meditation

Weekly

  • Dust
  • Wash bedding
  • Mop/Vacuum
  • Scrub Toilets/Sinks
  • Clean showers/tubs
  • Plan upcoming week and counsel with family

Bi-Weekly

  • Clean out fridge
  • Check outside of home for pests and cosmetic issues
  • Wipe down baseboards, doors, and appliances
  • Check food storage staples for rotation or refill

Monthly

  • Clean inside of washing machine and dishwasher (with cleaning solution or tablet through a cycle)
  • Sanitize all electronics, keyboards, lightswitches, and doorknobs.
  • Wash windows
  • Prep budget for upcoming month
  • Go through garage to sweep, declutter, and organize

Quarterly

  • Check sprinkler heads around home for broken heads and correct positioning
  • Check lightbulbs, dust around lamps or lights higher up
  • Clean range hood
  • Wipe down cupboards, inside and out
  • Clean out gutters
  • Go through closets to organize, declutter, and donate

Yearly

  • Check grout around home- does it need to be resealed or filled in?
  • Move appliances and furniture to clean floors underneath
  • Inspect smoke detectors
  • Check all emergency supplies for working batteries, lights, radios, expired food, etc.
  • Clean out dryer vents
  • Shampoo carpets
  • Check all personal care products for expiration (makeup, lotions, etc.)
  • Powerwash driveway and sides of home
